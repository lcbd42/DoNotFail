How to buy

Create a wallet

Download metamask or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to metamask.io.

Get Some BNB

Have BNB in your wallet to switch to $LADYS. If you don’t have any BNB, you can buy directly on metamask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

Go to Pancakeswap

Connect to pancakeswap. Go to pancakeswap.finance/swap?chain=bnb in google chrome or on the browser inside your Metamask app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $LADYS token address into pancakeswap, select Milady, and confirm. When Metamask prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.

Switch BNB for $LADYS

Switch BNB for $LADYS. We have ZERO taxes so you don’t need to worry about buying with a specific slippage, although you may need to use slippage during times of market volatility.
